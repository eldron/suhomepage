Bibliography

Jinshu Su is a professor of School of Computer, National University of Defense Technology. He is a leader of the Distributed Computing and High Performance Router Laboratory, the Computer Networks and Information Security Laboratory, and the High Performance Computer Networks Laboratory. His researches interests include network security and network architecture.
